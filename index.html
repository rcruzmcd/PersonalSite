About Me

My name is Ricardo Cruz Mcdougal and I am currently living in Miami, FL. Before I spend almost 2 years in Austin, TX and before almost 8 years in Charlotte, NC (and also about 8 month in NYC) so I have been in quite a few places. The rest of the time I spent in Dominican Republic where I was born. I earned my Bachelors from UNC Charlotte in Software and Information Systems with a concentration in Web Developpment. While leaving in Texas I worked as an ETL developer where I learned various technologies I did not even know about while finishing my undergraduate degree. For the last two months or so I have teamed up with a friend who is a Graphic designer and needed a Web Developer to take care of websites some of his clients wanted to get done.

In addition, I am currently enrolled at Boston University working towards a Master of Science in Computer Information Systems hopefully graduating in the Spring of 2018. I hope to sharpen my web development skills, and develop new ones to help me become a succesful Web Developer, both front and back end, in the industry.
